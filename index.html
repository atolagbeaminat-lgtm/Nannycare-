How We Verify Every Caregiver

Every nanny and elder companion goes through a rigorous online verification process before their profile goes live.

📝
Step 1 — Online Application

Complete your full profile — experience, languages, cultural background and certifications. Done entirely online.

🤖
Step 2 — AI Document Check

Upload your passport / ID and DBS certificate. Our AI-powered verification system checks authenticity automatically within 24 hours — no physical visit needed.

🎥
Step 3 — Online Video Interview

A short 20-minute video call with our team via Zoom — assessing your experience, communication and care values. Done from your home.

✅
Step 4 — Profile Live & Bookings Begin

Once approved, your profile goes live. Families book you. Earnings paid directly to your bank via Stripe Connect after every booking.

Our Founder

Aminat Atolagbe

Founder & Director, NannyCare Connect

"I searched everywhere for a nanny who could be more than just a caregiver — someone who could be a cultural bridge for my child. When I couldn't find one, I decided to build it."

As a Nigerian single mother and working professional, our founder experienced firsthand the challenge of finding childcare that truly understood her family's values, language and cultural identity — caring for a young child alone, with no family support nearby.

She also understood the pain of elderly parents far from home — people in care homes who feel culturally isolated, surrounded by people who don't speak their language or share their traditions and heritage.

NannyCare Connect was born from both of those lived experiences. Built not just as a business — but as a platform that gives every family dignity, connection and the care they truly deserve. Designed with multicultural and immigrant families at heart — and open to every family who values cultural connection, trust and personalised care.
